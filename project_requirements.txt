-target audience: students
-objective: allows users to record their expenses, categorize them, and view insights through charts or summaries.

# phase 1  
1. Add Daily Expenses
Each expense entry must include: Amount , Category (Food, Travel, Shopping,
Entertainment, Others, etc.) , Date , Short note/description (optional).

2. View All Expenses
A simple list/table showing: Date , Category , Amount , Notes (if any).Supports
pagination or scrolling.

4. Visual Insights (Charts)
Show the user how they spend their money using simple charts. Use any chart
library like: Chart.js or plotly etc.


# phase 2
3. Categorize & Filter
Allow users to: Filter expenses by category , Filter by date range (e.g., this
week, this month) , Search by keywords .

# phase 3

5. Basic Analytics (optional)
Display small insights such as: Total spent this month , Highest spending
category , Average daily spending etc.
